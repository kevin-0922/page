溝通推廣繪本

Communication Promotion Picture Book

「KEEP溝ING」出版一套關於中學生的溝通繪本，溝通這種學問，是在我們學習過程，常常被忽略的學問。因此我們將這個重要的學習放在國中，以國中生的生活人際關係來做故事主軸，因為人與人之間有時會因為溝通不良所產生誤會而引發爭執，成年人都會遇到這種事了，更何況是小孩，尤其是國中生，他們身處一個從小孩變成大人的時期，這個時期的學生多少有些自我，因此缺少與人溝通，所以我們想讓他們學著思考及反思自身，學會人與人相互溝通、理解，減少誤會的產生。

「KEEP溝ING」希望由兩位讀一起共同閱讀，一個事件、兩個視角，在閱讀完後可以一起討論關於此事件自己閱讀的視角的見解，做一個自身意見的討論、與分享。

"KEEP COMMUNICATING" has published a set of communication picture books aimed at middle school students. Communication is a discipline that is often overlooked in our learning process. We use the four interpersonal relationships of middle school students as the main story axis.

Misunderstandings and disputes can arise between people due to poor communication. This is a mistake that even adults make, let alone middle school students. They are at a time when they are transitioning from children to adults, and this period is often characterized by a lack of communication skills. If not taught properly, it can have negative consequences.

We hope that two readers can read together, with one event and two perspectives. After reading, they can discuss their own perspectives on the event, share their opinions, and communicate with each other through the story. This will help readers understand the event from another's perspective and achieve basic communication and exchange of ideas with their reading partners.

四種溝通

Four Communication

主視覺設計

Key vision

LOGO設計

標準字設計理念：以寬、圓的筆劃組成，將重複的兩個E相連，表達「持續進行」之意，中文字「溝」則融入逗號來呼應溝通的概念，筆劃交疊間融合了圓潤與稜角，表達不同的意見都能在溝通中匯集、交流。

設計理念

其實溝通不只是要主要說話，要注意的有五個關鍵的溝通技巧。分別為眼神、傾聽、溝通、肢體以及文字，所以我們製作了一本簡單的手冊，讓大家快速了解溝通的技巧。

視覺海報設計

理念-取自四本繪本之主題，將角色間溝通的概念納入海報中，並且表現對話的形式各有所不同。 此外再加入繪本系列裡的QA問答、擴音器與提倡溝通等概念圖像，使整體畫面更有變化性。

Design concept for standard characters: Composed of wide and round strokes, the repeated letter "E" is connected to express the concept of "continuous progress." The Chinese character "溝" (gou) incorporates a comma to correspond with the idea of communication. The overlapping strokes blend roundness and angularity to convey that different opinions can converge and communicate through dialogue.

In fact, communication is not just about speaking. There are five key communication skills to pay attention to, including eye contact, listening, verbal communication, body language, and written communication. Therefore, we have created a simple handbook to help everyone quickly understand the skills of communication.

Drawing from the themes of four picture books, the concept of communication between characters is incorporated into the poster, and different forms of dialogue are depicted. In addition, concepts such as QA Q&A, loudspeakers, and advocating communication from the picture book series are added to create a more varied visual.
